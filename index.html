Artist Statement: I created this art piece with one sole reason: To have fun. With many of the art pieces we see and learn about, a lot of them have an important, meaningful message. Comparable to Dadaism, I look to lightly challenge that. Why can't art just be made just to be art? Why can't it just exist where ever it may be? I created this art piece to just be something that's just there with no deeper meaning, just fun.
